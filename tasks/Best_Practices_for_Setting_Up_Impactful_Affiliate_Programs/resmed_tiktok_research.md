# ResMed TikTok Shop Affiliate Program — Research Notes

## TikTok Shop Affiliate Program Overview

### Two Core Collaboration Types (TikTok Seller University)
1. **Target Collaboration** — Invite-only. Sellers invite specific creators to promote selected products. Commission rates set individually or for groups. Best for niche/technical products requiring brand alignment.
2. **Open Collaboration** — Products visible to all eligible creators. Options for approval or fully open. Best for broad reach and awareness building.
3. **VIP Collaboration** (emerging) — Exclusive partnership with 1–2 high-GMV or celebrity creators. Upfront payment + commission. Acts as a "hype cycle" driver.

### When to Use Each
- **New to TikTok Shop** → Start with targeted collabs; craft compelling outreach to build initial creator base
- **Established brand** → Open collabs benefit from brand awareness among creators
- **Niche/technical products** (like ResMed sleep devices) → Targeted is better; ensures creators understand and can credibly sell the product
- **Core high-performers** → Reserve targeted for your "army of historically performance-driven creators" at higher commission rates

## Creator Quality & Vetting Best Practices

### Key Quality Signals (Superfiliate / Zeroto1)
- **5,000–10,000 followers** as a minimum threshold (not mega-influencers)
- **High activation rate** — content posted based on products received (>80% "content completion rate")
- **Prior brand content history** — has made content for other brands before
- **Engagement rate** over follower count — "half a million followers but only 1,000 views" is a red flag
- **GMV granularity** — was GMV driven by 1 viral video or consistent output? Was it paid or organic?
- **Posting cadence** — recent activity matters more than historical peaks

### TikTok/BSI Creator Suitability Framework (March 2026 Report)
Three layers:
1. **Safety** — Non-negotiable: past racism, violence, criminal activity, harmful behavior = exclude
2. **Suitability** — Current content tone, audience expectations, brand positioning alignment
3. **Relevance** — Does the partnership make sense to the creator's own community?

Key finding: **Follower count ranked LAST** among brand selection factors (8% for brands, 7% for agencies). Suitability/fit ranked #1 (22%).

**Risk spectrum for branded content:**
- In-feed brand ads = lowest adjacency risk
- Creator profile adjacency = medium risk
- Creator-produced branded content = highest exposure (requires most vetting)

**Frequency amplifies risk**: 28% of consumers need 3–4 exposures before purchase. Misalignment compounds over time.

### Creator Vetting Red Flags for Health/Medical Brands
- Unsubstantiated health claims in past content
- Promoting competing or conflicting health products
- Low content completion rate (posts fewer than 80% of products received)
- Inconsistent posting cadence
- Engagement/follower ratio mismatch

## Creator Sourcing Without Manual Overload

### The Scale Problem
- ~750,000 registered TikTok Shop affiliates
- Manual swiping is not scalable
- Every brand is competing for the same creator pool

### Solutions
1. **TikTok Creator Marketplace** — Filter by product category, GMV, follower count, engagement
2. **Superfiliate Autopilot** — Set eligibility criteria; auto-sends target collaboration invites daily to matching creators
3. **Bulk invite** — Up to 400 creators at once via target collaboration
4. **Cross-channel sourcing** — Identify creators performing on Instagram/YouTube and recruit them to TikTok Shop
5. **Open collab with approval gate** — Let creators apply, then approve only those meeting criteria (balance of scale + quality)
6. **Product seeding** — Send samples to micro-creators; track content completion rate to identify performers

### Commission Strategy to Attract Quality Creators
- Set competitive base commission rates in open collabs
- Offer higher "exclusive" commission rates in targeted collabs for top performers
- Use tiered commissions: reward creators who hit GMV milestones with higher rates
- Flat fee + commission for VIP/exclusive partnerships

## TikTok Shop Setup Best Practices for Brands

### Phase 1: Foundation
1. Set up TikTok Shop Seller Center
2. Optimize product listings (clear visuals, health claims compliant)
3. Establish organic posting cadence BEFORE recruiting affiliates (creators vet brands too)
4. Set commission rates: open collab baseline + targeted collab premium rates

### Phase 2: Creator Acquisition
1. Start with targeted collabs to a curated list of 50–100 health/wellness creators
2. Use Creator Marketplace filters: health/wellness category, 5K–100K followers, prior GMV
3. Send product samples with clear brief and brand guidelines
4. Track content completion rate; only continue with >80% performers
5. Gradually open to open collaboration with approval gate

### Phase 3: Quality Control Without Manual Overload
1. **Pre-approval criteria**: Set minimum follower count, engagement rate, content category filters
2. **Brand brief template**: Provide clear dos/don'ts, approved claims, messaging guidelines
3. **Content approval workflow**: For health/medical brands, require content review before posting (or post-posting monitoring)
4. **Blocklist/exclusion**: Use TikTok's Video Exclusion Lists and Profile Feed Exclusion Lists
5. **Autopilot tools**: Use Superfiliate or similar to automate daily invite sending

### Phase 4: Amplification
1. Identify top-performing affiliate videos (organic GMV, engagement, completion rate)
2. Get permission from creators to use as Spark Ads / Video Shopping Ads
3. Use GMV Max to automate ad optimization across catalog
4. Reinvest commission savings from automation into higher rates for top creators

## Health Brand-Specific Considerations (ResMed Context)

### Regulatory/Compliance
- Sleep apnea devices are regulated medical devices — affiliate content must not make unsubstantiated claims
- Creators must disclose affiliate relationships (#ad, #sponsored)
- TikTok 2025 policy: posting limits for low-quality affiliate content (5+ non-interactive posts = restrictions)
- FTC disclosure rules apply

### Creator Profile for ResMed
- Sleep health advocates, sleep coaches, respiratory therapists
- Wellness/health creators with credible health audiences
- CPAP/sleep apnea community creators
- Lifestyle creators focused on sleep optimization, biohacking, wellness routines

### Messaging Guardrails
- Focus on sleep quality, energy, daily performance benefits
- Avoid specific medical claims ("cures sleep apnea")
- Emphasize comfort, ease of use, lifestyle integration
- User testimonials framed as personal experience, not medical advice
