**What is a Next Best Action (NBA) Framework?**

Imagine you have a really smart friend who knows everything about you. They know what you like, what you've already tried, and what you might need next. Instead of just randomly suggesting things, they always give you the *perfect* suggestion at the *perfect* time.

That's basically what an NBA framework is — but for a company talking to its customers.

Right now, ResMed runs ads and sends messages kind of like a megaphone — the same message goes out to a lot of people at once. An NBA framework is more like a personal assistant for each customer. It looks at what that person has done (did they take the sleep quiz? did they click an ad? did they almost buy something?) and then decides: *"What's the best thing to say to THIS person, RIGHT NOW, to help them take the next step?"*

---

**The Problem We're Solving (in simple terms)**

Think of it like a lemonade stand. ResMed has gotten really good at getting people to walk up to the stand and try a free sample (that's the Sleep Assessment — 68,000 people did it this year!). But only 698 of those people actually bought a cup of lemonade.

So the question is: **what happened to the other 67,000+ people who tried the sample and then just... walked away?**

Right now, nobody really knows. Maybe they needed a nudge. Maybe they had a question. Maybe they forgot. The NBA framework figures out *why* each person walked away and sends them exactly the right message to bring them back.

---

**The 3-Step Plan (super simple version)**

**Month 1 — Be a detective 🔍**
First, we figure out what currently happens after someone takes the sleep quiz. Do they get an email? A phone call? Nothing? We map the whole journey so we know where people are getting lost.

**Months 2–3 — Build the brain 🧠**
We connect all the clues together — the quiz data, the website visits, the ad clicks — so we can start to understand each person. Then we pick one simple test: *"Can we turn more quiz-completers into buyers?"*

**Months 4–6 — Send the right message 📬**
We use what we learned to send each person a personalized message — the right words, through the right channel (email, text, ad), at the right time. Then we measure whether it worked and keep improving.

---

**The big idea in one sentence:**

> Instead of shouting the same thing at everyone, we whisper exactly the right thing to each person — and that's how ResMed turns 68,000 free samples into a lot more lemonade sales. 🍋
