# Key Takeaways + Next Steps | Q1 2026 - Social

## Key Takeaways - Social
1. **Positive Trends**
    a. Q1 marked a massive unlock in high-intent demand generation, driven by the launch of Competitor Prospecting campaigns which successfully captured market share and drove highly efficient RAQs.
    b. The launch of the "Where to Go" campaigns at the end of March proved instantly successful, driving RABs at exceptionally low costs and efficiently engaging our target high-net-worth audience.
    c. Geo-expansion efforts scaled our footprint across 25 active countries, while our 2-minute Time on Site campaigns delivered over 2M+ highly-engaged sessions, proving our creative is capturing luxury audience attention.
2. **Areas of Improvement**
    a. While overall lead volume and efficiency were strong, CRM retargeting campaigns showed softer performance compared to prospecting efforts, reinforcing the need to refresh our retargeting strategies.
3. **Insights from testing**
    a. The introduction of new UGC assets drove strong engagement and proved highly effective at moving users down the funnel, signaling that authentic, user-driven creative resonates strongly with our audience.
    b. Shifting strategy to explicitly target competitor audiences yielded some of the most efficient RAQ conversions of the quarter, proving the value of aggressive conquesting in the luxury cruise space.

## Next Steps
1. **Optimizations**
    a. Continue to monitor the success and scale of the "Where to Go" campaigns to maximize RAB volume and efficiency.
    b. Expand the successful "Where to Go" assets to RAQ campaigns, developing a custom landing page similar to the current RAB experience to improve lead quality and conversion rates.
2. **Testing Strategy**
    a. Launch Lookalike (LAL) prospecting for RAQ, building on the massive success of the Competitor Prospecting campaigns to find net-new high-intent users.
    b. Plan to test primary-guest versus all-guest lookalike seeds across RAB, aiming to identify the optimal balance between lead quality, efficiency, and scale.
    c. Test into X specifically to reach HNWI in APAC.
3. **Creative Workflow**
    a. Maximize on the creative testing and results:
        i. Continue scaling new UGC experience story assets, as they are driving strong conversion results.
        ii. Deepen our destination-specific assets by building out itinerary-style carousels.
        iii. Conde Nast assets on LinkedIn still pending approval but foresee being strong assets.
